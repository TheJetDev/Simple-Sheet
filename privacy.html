Privacy Policy

App Name: Simple Sheet

1. Information Collection and Cloud Storage

"Simple Sheet" (hereinafter referred to as "this app") generally stores all calculation data and memos locally on the user's device. We do not collect personal information (names, addresses, etc.).

Share Feature: When you use the "Share" or "QR Code" features, the specific calculation data (title, rows, and memo) you choose to share is uploaded to Google Firebase servers. This data is used solely to generate a shareable link and is automatically deleted after 30 days. This data is not linked to your personal identity.

This app offers paid features via the Google Play Billing system. All transactions are processed securely by Google. This app does not collect your payment details.

We may collect anonymous data (crash reports, install counts) via Google Play Console to improve app quality. This data is non-identifiable.
