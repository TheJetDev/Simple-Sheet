Privacy Policy

App Name: Simple Sheet

1. Account and Personal Data

Simple Sheet does not require user registration or accounts. We do not collect or store personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2. Data Storage and Cloud Services

Local Storage: In principle, all calculation data and memos are stored only on the user's device (or within the browser's local storage for the web version).

Share Feature: When you use the "Share" or "QR Code" features, the specific calculation data you choose to share is uploaded to Google Firebase (provided by Google) servers. This data is used solely to generate a temporary link and is automatically deleted after 30 days. This data is not associated with your personal identity.

Copy All Feature (Pro): Data generated by the "Copy All" feature is processed entirely on your device and is not uploaded to any server. This allows for safe, offline data transfer.

3. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the content they choose to share. Please avoid sharing sensitive, personal, or confidential information. The developer reserves the right to delete shared data if it is deemed inappropriate or violates content guidelines.

4. In-App Purchases

All transactions are processed securely through the Google Play Billing system. This app does not collect or have access to your payment details.

5. Disclaimer

The developer is not responsible for any damages arising from the use of this app or shared links. While we strive for accuracy, calculation results are not guaranteed.
